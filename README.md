# Am I Cooked?

A chaotic life simulator where every decision affects just how cooked you are.

## What's This?

I wanted to make something fun instead of another serious productivity app, so I created **Am I Cooked?**. The game throws random embarrassing situations at you and gives you choices on how to respond. Depending on your decisions, your Cooked Level goes up, and by the end you'll find out whether you survived or are absolutely finished.

## What You Can Do

* Play through a series of random life disasters
* Choose how you react to each situation
* Build up your Cooked Level based on your decisions
* Earn a final grade and ending
* Replay and see if you can make better choices

## Example Scenarios

* You called the teacher "mom."
* You got crossed in basketball and everyone started yelling.
* You waved at someone who wasn't waving at you.
* Your alarm didn't go off and you're already 40 minutes late.
* Your friend asked for honesty and then got mad at the answer.

## How to Play

1. Click **Start Game**.
2. Read the scenario.
3. Choose one of the available responses.
4. Continue through all scenarios.
5. Receive your final Cooked Score, grade, and ending.

## Why I Made This

The goal of this project was to make something entertaining and a little ridiculous. Everyone has embarrassing moments, so I thought it would be funny to turn them into a game and see how badly things could go depending on the choices you make.
